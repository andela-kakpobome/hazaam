Music FPaaS

Fingerprint as a Service

What is FPaaS?

No, it does not involve fingers :) .
Songs have a unique feel, sound and identity, similar to fingerprints. With the fingerprint as a service (FPaaS), you can generate a digital representation of any song.

How Does It Work?

When your song is uploaded to our platform, the fingerprint generator computes time and hash pairs from the audio signal using advanced signal processing to account for noise and modification. For more details, find link here and here.
